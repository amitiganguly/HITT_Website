Mission Statement

At HIIT (Human Inquiry Into Tech), we bridge the gap between technological advancement and human understanding. We're a community of curious minds who believe that the best conversations about technology don't just focus on what's possible, but what's right. Our mission is to explore the human impact of emerging technologies through open dialogue, critical thinking, and diverse perspectives. From artificial intelligence to biotechnology, we tackle the complex questions that arise when innovation meets ethics. We believe that everyone - regardless of their technical background - has valuable insights to share about how technology shapes our lives and society. Together, we're working to ensure that as technology evolves, it does so in harmony with human values, serving not just progress for progress's sake, but the genuine betterment of all people and communities. Through monthly discussions, workshops, and collaborative projects, we're building a future where technological advancement and ethical consideration go hand in hand.
