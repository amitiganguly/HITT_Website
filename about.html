About Us

We believe technology is more than just circuits and code – it's the story of human progress, creativity, and connection. At Human Inquiry Into Tech, we explore the fascinating and complex relationship between innovation and ethics. As technology reshapes our world, from AI chatbots to genetic breakthroughs, we ask the essential questions: What makes technology 'good'? How do we ensure it serves everyone, not just a select few? Where do we draw the lines between progress and preservation of human values? Through engaging discussions and diverse perspectives, we create a space where students, professionals, and curious minds come together to untangle these challenges. We're not just passive observers – we're active participants in shaping how technology can build a future that's both innovative and inclusive. Join us in exploring the human side of the digital age, where every voice contributes to understanding how we can harness technology's potential while staying true to our shared values.

New Technologies

In today's rapidly evolving world, emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, and Quantum Computing are reshaping industries and societies. Our club keeps a close eye on these advancements, analyzing both their capabilities and their potential risks.

Artificial Intelligence

AI has the potential to revolutionize industries. We focus on its ethical implications, from bias in algorithms to accountability in decision-making.

Blockchain Technology

Blockchain is disrupting finance, supply chains, and more. We explore the ethical and regulatory challenges posed by decentralized systems.

Quantum Computing

Quantum computing could solve problems once deemed impossible, but we examine the potential risks related to privacy and national security.

Ethics in Technology

As technology progresses, so do the ethical dilemmas that accompany it. We believe it is crucial to tackle these issues head-on to ensure that innovation benefits all of humanity. Our focus is on responsible technology development, advocating for privacy, transparency, and accountability.

Privacy & Data Security

In an era of mass data collection, privacy has become a critical issue. We examine the ethical implications of data harvesting and user consent.

Bias

Artificial intelligence systems can perpetuate existing biases. Our club works on addressing fairness in AI algorithms to ensure equity
